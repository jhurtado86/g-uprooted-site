What Tree Removal Involves — and When You Need It

Some trees can be saved with a good trim. Others are past that point — and waiting only makes them more dangerous. A dead, dying, or storm-damaged tree can drop heavy limbs without warning, lean toward your roof, or come down in the next big Rio Grande Valley storm. Tree removal takes the hazard down safely, before it turns into property damage or an injury.

Our team handles the whole job from start to finish. We look at the tree, check what's around it, and plan the safest way down. When a tree stands close to a house, fence, driveway, or power line, we take it apart piece by piece instead of just dropping it — then we haul off the wood and debris and leave the spot clean. We do this for homes and businesses across Harlingen, McAllen, Brownsville, and the surrounding RGV every week.

Not every tree needs to come out, and we'll tell you straight. If a trim or some cabling will keep it healthy and standing, we'll say so. When removal really is the right call, you get an honest, upfront price before any work starts — and the estimate is always free.

Questions About Tree Removal

How long does tree removal take?
Most residential tree removals are done in a few hours, start to finish. A very large tree, a tight spot next to a house, or a full commercial job can take longer. When you call, we'll give you a clear timeframe so you can plan your day.
How much does tree removal cost in Harlingen?
Every tree is different — size, location, and how close it is to a house or power line all affect the price. That's why we give you a free, upfront quote before any work starts, with no hidden fees. Call us with the details (a few photos help) and we'll walk you through it.
Do you remove the stump too?
We can. Taking down a tree leaves a stump behind, and grinding it out keeps your yard usable and pest-free. Ask about tree stump removal and grinding when you book and we'll handle both in one visit.
What if a tree comes down in a storm?
Call us right away — we offer 24/7 storm damage and emergency tree service across the Rio Grande Valley. If a tree is on your roof, blocking a driveway, or leaning on a power line, our team responds fast to make the area safe.
How soon can you get to me?
Same-day and next-day appointments are often available across Harlingen and the Rio Grande Valley, and emergencies are handled 24/7. Call us and we'll find the soonest time that works for you.
