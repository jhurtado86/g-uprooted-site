About G-Uprooted Tree Services

Owner Emilio Gonzalez has spent more than a decade trimming and removing trees and palms across the Rio Grande Valley. G-Uprooted is the crew RGV homeowners, property managers, and businesses call when they want the job done right — and the yard left clean.

Our Story — Meet Emilio

G-Uprooted Tree Services is owner Emilio Gonzalez's company, and it carries his name and his standards on every job. For more than 10 years, Emilio has been up in the trees and palms of the Rio Grande Valley — learning how each one grows here, how to cut it right, and how to bring a big one down safely when it's time.

That experience shows up in the work. We don't top trees or over-cut them to make a quick dollar — bad cuts weaken a tree and leave it more dangerous than before. We prune to keep trees healthy, take out only what needs to go, and always put your property and the people around it first. No job is too big or too small, and the estimate is always free.

Emilio built G-Uprooted to serve the whole Valley — homeowners who want a clean, healthy yard, and the property managers, RV parks, HOAs, and real estate companies that need a dependable crew on call. Commercial or residential, you get the same straight answers and the same careful work.

Based in Harlingen and serving McAllen, Brownsville, South Padre Island, Edinburg, Mission, and the surrounding RGV, G-Uprooted feels less like a faceless company and more like calling someone who'll actually pick up. We're also here around the clock — 24/7 — when a storm brings a tree down and you need it cleared fast.
